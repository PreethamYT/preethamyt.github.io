### Why "Conscious Coder"?

Simple: I want to be a *deliberate* programmer, someone who understands what they are building and why, not just someone who copies Stack Overflow answers until something compiles.

Most people learn to code reactively. A tutorial here, a crash course there, whatever gets the assignment done. I want to do the opposite, slow down, ask first principles questions, and actually understand the craft.

The name is also a reminder to myself. When I open an editor, I want it to be with intention, not just to fill time.
