## Hey, I'm Preetham Rao.
I'm a 2nd-year CS student at GRIET, Hyderabad.

This blog is where I process my journey to understand one thing: How do you actually get better?

Not just accumulating skills. But thinking about HOW you learn, WHY you fail, WHAT sticks.

Most people grind. I'm here to understand the grind.

### What You'll Find Here:
- Reflections on what I learned (and what I got wrong)
- DSA breakdowns - the thinking, not just solutions
- Project failures and lessons
- First-principles thoughts on growth, learning, and building with intention

### Why "Conscious Coder"?
Simple: I want to be deliberate. Not just copying Stack Overflow until it compiles. 
Not just following tutorials to fill time.

I want to understand the craft. The process. How real growth actually happens.

This blog is where I slow down and write that out.
