So yes, this is my first blog post.

Anyways,

---

This team contest, my first team contest, was held on 25th Feb 2026, On a Wednesday, The weather was kinda cool and calming (kind odd since it's the start of the summer), The maximum team size was set to 5, and the least was set to 2, by our Beloved Aniketh Sir from Smart Interviews (Contest was from their side too), I think it's time I introduce what Smart Interviews is doing there, So our college has hired Smart Interviews to train the students at our college for training, moving on...My team....Yeah 4 of us in total, so One guy sticks to Python, One guy knows literally Java, CPP AND Python(a lil), Java, and me, CPP(just starting), Python,and Java ( Not my primary choice though ).

---

So we had our regular Theory session in the morning, and post lunch we were informed we would be having the Team contest, during our (team's) lunch we were brainstorming what could be the team name...Taking names from our class friends, a few animes (lmao), so we were thinking "The Straw Hats", "Black Bulls", "Team 7" Kinda names.....thinking back to it, If we would've spent this time on revising the material we had we PROBABLY would've scored better 😂

---

![Contest day](/assets/images/posts/Team-Contest.png)

---

Now it's 1:20 pm the contest had started, we had created the team on our Smart Interviews platform as well, total scoreable points are 500, Total 6 Problems, Looking at my teammates (I had attended quite a few hackathons with them and know how they operate for the most part, but me being me I was hoping they had probably adopted a new way of working, was i right?)

So the contest started, 6 Questions, we had seen every question, marked which was easy, which was medium, which was hard, and we were next onto alloting the questions to the person who wanted to solve them.

I took 2nd question, another friend took another question so on...

So, my question I'm solving, my friend next to me, I swear, he's looking at what I'm solving, I see his paper, and He's literally trying to solve my question when he was tasked with doing the previous question!!! I asked him, "Did you wanna switch?" He said no... So I again told "bruh just do ur question 😭"

---

Fine we're done with that friend, another friend, what he did, He re-wrote the SAME code I had written AFTER commenting MY code, mind you he volunteerly took another question, and we both had implemented the brute force, so IDK what was going on in his mind but...idk at the pace our team was working, it got to me, I was really upset, looked at our team rank, it was 36th (out of 44 teams) I usually code in Python coupled with my insane typing speed I submit quicker compared to others, and Python also doesn't need me to write the brackets EVERY time I have a for loop or an if condition so it's quicker, usually my rank is in the better part, ok alright we were doing bad, I said my team guys this is our rank...uk what a guy said?, "It doesn't matter man", He said this exact same thing to me after we lost yet another hackathon...Bro if ur reading this just see my pov 😭, I'm highly competitive, so hearing that mid-contest honestly fried my brain. I realized right then that not everyone operates with the same intensity or cares about the leaderboard the way I do. If I want to win, I have to figure out how to work with different mindsets, not just expect everyone to have the same fire 😭😭😭

---

So the first question which was given to that guy, his java code had failed, So I had to do that again, in Python (Since my cpp isn't as sharp as my Python yet), then I did my 3rd Question, moved on...

The team was also having their on set of struggles, their whole pressure, I mean usually One guy in our team does fantastic, he understands few approaches which even I don't understand to this date and I work on it, HE was doing bad, Maybe the pressure got to him, idk...But the main lesson what I learnt that day was everybody has their own style, my Python friend was trying to come up with Greedy Approaches, hence he struggled with Backtracking, so everyone has their own Strengths and Weaknesses, got to understand them and play accordingly, also It would have been NICE if everybody on my team (including me) had revised a few concepts during lunch instead of brainstorming team names, oh yeah we decided on the "Silver Eagles" as a team name btw 😇🙏🏻 (Inspired from Black Clover),

---

Yet despite all our valiant efforts, we had done prettyyyyy bad in the contest as a team (We came 39th). There IS scope for improvement, and that is where we can improve. I hope all of my teammates have also reflected upon our efforts and analysed where they had gone wrong ( that includes me ), and do better next time :)

That's it, folks!

See ya next time!
