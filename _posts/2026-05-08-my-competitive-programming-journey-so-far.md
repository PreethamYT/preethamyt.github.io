so it's been a WHILE since I've written my last blog y'all, and a LOT has happened, my 2-2 Exams are around the corner, and the placements are supposedly starting next semester itself,

I have been venturing into this world of "Competitive Programming", with even my own college having the training program I should be able to do CP ( I'm just gonna call Competitive Programming as CP for the rest of the blog ) pretty well right? Let's see.

So my current CodeForces rating is 808, and LeetCode is around 1,493 With CodeChef being 1283, If you ask anyone from the hiring unit, these are probably some terrible stats for like the past 1, 1 and half month I've been following Dr Mostafa's beginners sheet, I can see steady improvements in the way I solve problems and the way I go about looking at problems as well, and grinding CF so far has been one of the most amazing experiences in my life!

![Codeforces Profile](/assets/images/posts/cf-profile.png)

The CP community is so fun and welcoming, they don't discourage u, they don't look down just cause you didn't solve a problem, that itself to me is so fascinating like what kind of people are actually in here, so nice and welcoming I definitely wanted to be a CPer(Competitive Programmer) after seeing the community and switching to C++ from python.

and CONTESTS!! I've noticed myself improving my solving speed over the weeks, honestly the 3rd qs of CodeChef used to take me atleast 30 mins, Now I can do it in 5. But since I wasn't upsolving I still can't seem to figure out the 4th Question even after spending like 1 and half hour on it, literally.
